Ergonomics & Performance

Professional Bike Setup

Harmony between man and machine. The perfect ride starts with the perfect fit.

A bicycle is a precision machine, but it only performs at its best when it's perfectly tuned to your body mechanics. Riding a bike that hasn't been set up for you can lead to power loss, discomfort, and even long-term injury.

Our setup service goes beyond basic saddle height adjustments. We analyze your flexibility, riding style, and biomechanics to create a position that maximizes power output while eliminating discomfort. Whether you are a weekend warrior or a competitive racer, the right fit unlocks your full potential.

The Setup Process:

  • Saddle Position: Precise height and fore/aft adjustment for optimal leg extension and power transfer.
  • Reach & Drop: Stem length and handlebar rotation adjustments to reduce back and neck strain.
  • Cleat Alignment: Ensuring your knees track correctly to prevent injury and improve pedal efficiency.
